Our Mission

To recognize, empower, and celebrate the everyday trailblazers who spark meaningful, lasting impact in their communities. Icons of Change is dedicated to elevating the unrecognized changemakers by providing them a dynamic platform for visibility, connection, and growth opportunities. Through innovative awards, forward-thinking training, and collaborative programs, we aim to ignite a culture of sustainable impact and inspire game-changing partnerships across the public and private sectors, fueling transformation in society.


Our Vision

A world where every community leader and changemaker is acknowledged, empowered, and equipped to drive sustainable impact. Icons of Change envisions a collaborative ecosystem that bridges communities, organizations, and global institutions, promoting strategic partnerships that inspire positive, lasting transformation across the Philippines and beyond. United by shared purpose, these empowered changemakers will lead resilient, empowered communities for a better future.

Who We Are

Icons of Change is your platform for celebrating everyday trailblazers who create meaningful, lasting change. Through awards, training, and collaborative programs, we elevate unsung heroes and inspire game-changing partnerships across sectors.
