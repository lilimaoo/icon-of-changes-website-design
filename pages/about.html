Our Mission

To recognize, empower, and celebrate the everyday trailblazers who spark meaningful, lasting impact in their communities. Icons of Change is dedicated to elevating the unrecognized changemakers by providing them a dynamic platform for visibility, connection, and growth opportunities. Through innovative awards, forward-thinking training, and collaborative projects, we aim to foster a culture of sustainable impact and inspire innovative collaborations across public and private sectors that drive social change.

Our Vision

A world where every community leader and changemaker is acknowledged, empowered, and equipped to drive sustainable impact. Icons of Change envisions a collaborative ecosystem that bridges communities, organizations, and global institutions, fostering strategic partnerships that inspire positive, lasting change throughout the Philippines and beyond.

United under a common purpose, these empowered changemakers will lead resilient, empowered communities toward a better future.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Icons of Change

Innovation

We embrace creative thinking and novel approaches to addressing complex social challenges, constantly seeking to improve our methods and maximize our impact.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnerships and collective action, working across sectors and communities to achieve greater impact than any single entity could alone.

Equity

We are committed to fairness and justice in all our work, ensuring that opportunities and resources are accessible to all, particularly those who have been historically marginalized.

Sustainability

We pursue solutions that create lasting change, considering long-term environmental, social, and economic impacts in all our decisions and actions.

Empathy

We approach our work with compassion and understanding, respecting the dignity, experiences, and perspectives of all people we engage with.

Integrity

We hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards, operating with transparency, honesty, and accountability in all our interactions and initiatives.
