Our Team

Doctor

Alberto Dilao Madrigal

Dr. Alberto Dilao Madrigal is a transformative leader in education and public service, whose dedication to academic excellence and ethical leadership has profoundly impacted institutions and communities. With nearly two decades of experience in localizing higher education, he has played a key role in pioneering academic reforms, strengthening governance, and expanding access to quality education. As the co-founder of the City College of Calamba and former University President of Pamantasan ng Cabuyao, his leadership helped secure historic accreditation milestones and national subsidies, ensuring sustainable growth for local colleges and universities. Beyond academia, Dr. Madrigal has been an advocate for ethical governance, instilling values-driven leadership among government officials and law enforcement officers. His influence extends internationally as a sought-after speaker, inspiring individuals to embrace education, integrity, and service as pillars of positive societal change.

Engineer

Peter James Agada

Peter James Agada is a distinguished academic professional with extensive experience as an Academic Specialist, Quality Assurance and Special Projects Officer, and College Professor at Southville International School and Colleges. His career reflects a deep commitment to educational excellence, demonstrated through his mentoring of students, advising classes, and managing programs. As an Engineering professor, Peter has excelled in leadership roles, promoting innovation and development in academic and professional settings. His dedication to quality assurance and special projects displays his ability to drive impactful initiatives that enhance learning outcomes and institutional success.

John Guarnes

John Guarnes is a renowned Sustainable Fashion Designer in the Philippines, and a visionary leader whose advocacy transforms lives by creating job opportunities for the underprivileged. His work exemplifies a commitment to advancing Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly Goal 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth) and Goal 10 (Reduced Inequalities). Through his innovative designs and community-focused initiatives, John empowers marginalized individuals, promoting economic resilience and social inclusion.

Justin Ho

Justin Ho Guo Shun is an accomplished startup mentor and consultant with over ten years of experience across diverse industries, including sustainability, education, venture capital, and social enterprise work. His expertise spans leadership, entrepreneurship, and community building, making him a key figure in initiatives that bridge complex international and multi-stakeholder environments. Justin specializes in mentoring topics such as strategic human resource planning, corporate governance, sustainability, and startup ecosystem development. He is highly regarded for driving sustainable change and promoting responsible business practices while maintaining a pragmatic approach to resolving complex issues​.
