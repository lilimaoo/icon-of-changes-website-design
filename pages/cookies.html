Cookies Policy

Effective Date: March 2020

At Icons of Change, we believe your digital experience should be as clean, transparent, and empowering as the stories we tell. That's why this website is proudly cookie-light.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites to remember preferences, analyze traffic, or enhance usability. They're like the digital equivalent of a Post-it note—helpful in some cases, intrusive in others.

2. Our Cookie Practices

Currently, Icons of Change does not use cookies for tracking, personalization, analytics, or advertising. You can explore, read, and reflect on our changemakers' stories without your browser leaving a breadcrumb trail.

We do not:

  • Use session or persistent cookies
  • Collect behavioral data
  • Serve personalized ads or third-party tracking scripts

Simply put: no cookies, no complications.

3. Embedded Content

Some pages may feature embedded content (e.g., YouTube videos, social media posts) from third-party platforms. These providers may set cookies as part of their standard behavior—even if you don't interact with the content.

We recommend reviewing the cookie policies of these third-party services if you're concerned about tracking beyond our platform.

4. Managing Cookies on Your Device

Although we don't use cookies, your browser might. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. If you're a privacy purist—more power to you.

5. Future Changes

If our cookie practices change (e.g., to enhance site performance or introduce interactive features), this policy will be updated accordingly, with full transparency and compliance.

Final Word

Icons of Change is committed to offering a respectful, secure, and inspiration-driven digital space. Your attention is the only thing we're interested in capturing.

For questions, clarifications, or cookie-related curiosities, contact us at theiconsofchange@gmail.com
