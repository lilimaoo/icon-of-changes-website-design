Icons of Change Symposium: International Year of Peace and Trust

SM Center Las Piñas, Las Piñas City, Philippines

January 25, 2025 | 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Event by Icons of Change

Public · Anyone can attend

In celebration of the United Nations General Assembly's proclamation of 2025 as the International Year of Peace and Trust, Icons of Change brings together exceptional leaders who have made remarkable contributions to promoting unity, building trust, and sustainable development. This event will not only highlight the significance of the UN declaration but also inspire action at the local and national levels to create a more peaceful and trusting society.

What the United Nations Proclamation Means

The International Year of Peace and Trust is a global call to prioritize dialogue, cooperation, and solidarity. It recognizes that lasting peace and trust can only be achieved by addressing the root causes of conflict, strengthening justice and equality, and promoting partnerships that transcend borders, cultures, and ideologies. This year challenges us to take actionable steps in our communities to uphold these values and lay the groundwork for a better future.

Meet the Speakers

Felipe Fernández Montealto Jr.

Felipe has built a life dedicated to justice, advocacy, and meaningful service. With extensive experience across legal, governmental, and community sectors, he brings a unique perspective to the challenge of building trust and peace in today's society.

How He Contributes to the UN's Vision:

- Advocating for Justice and Human Dignity: Felipe's historic victory in the Supreme Court case ISDI v. Montealto Jr., which upheld the rights of Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs), demonstrates his commitment to fairness and equality.

- Community Leadership: As President of the Fondazione Vatican Giovanni Paolo II Philippine Chapter, Felipe actively works to unite communities through faith-based initiatives, promoting dialogue, compassion, and shared values.

- Governance and Policy Reform: His tenure as Chair Emeritus of the Philippine National Police EOD K9 Advisory Group reflects his ability to contribute to peacekeeping efforts, enhancing public trust in law enforcement.

Dr. Angelo E. Base

A visionary in the fields of law, mental health, and education, Dr. Base combines his expertise to create meaningful impact in the lives of individuals and communities. His work reflects the interconnectedness of peace, mental well-being, and ethical governance.

How He Contributes to the UN's Vision:

- Conflict Resolution: As a Supreme Court mediator and co-founder of BD&A Mediation Services Philippines, Dr. Base has helped resolve countless disputes through fair and effective mediation.

- Empowering Future Leaders: As Dean of Pearl of the Orient Theological Seminary & Colleges Inc., he nurtures a new generation of leaders grounded in ethics, compassion, and a commitment to community.

- Mental Health Advocacy: His work as a behavioral therapist shows the critical role of mental health in creating stable, harmonious societies.

Euan Rex Toralballa

A dynamic leader with a passion for empowering communities, Euan has dedicated his career to promoting progress in Las Piñas and beyond. His experience spans leadership roles in business, community development, and governance.

How He Contributes to the UN's Vision:

- Economic Empowerment: As Regional Head for Small Businesses at PLDT Enterprise, Euan has helped countless entrepreneurs thrive, emphasizing the importance of economic opportunities in reducing inequality.

- Community Leadership: Euan's work as President of the BFRV Homeowners Association Inc. demonstrates his ability to unite diverse stakeholders, resolve local issues, and promote community welfare.

- Innovative Problem-Solving: Euan's leadership in the private sector, including his roles at Smart Communications and Banco de Oro, equips him with the tools to develop innovative solutions to societal challenges.

Why You Should Join

The Icons of Change Symposium offers a unique opportunity to learn from these distinguished leaders and gain practical insights into how we can contribute to the UN's vision in our own communities. Expect thought-provoking discussions on topics such as:

- Promoting justice and reducing inequality as foundations for peace

- Addressing mental health and well-being to strengthen community trust

- Empowering local economies to reduce disparities and promote collaboration

- Uniting diverse groups through dialogue and shared values

Together for a Peaceful and Trusting World

As we embark on the International Year of Peace and Trust, let us draw inspiration from leaders like Felipe Fernández Montealto Jr., Dr. Angelo E. Base, and Euan Rex Toralballa. Their work reminds us that achieving peace and trust is not just a lofty ideal but a collective responsibility.

Join us on January 25, 2025, at SM Center Las Piñas, as we celebrate this global initiative and explore how we can create a future rooted in harmony, cooperation, and mutual respect.

Icons of Change Metro Manila 2025 Awarding Ceremony

Southville International School and Colleges, Las Piñas City, Philippines

April 27, 2025 | 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Event by Icons of Change

Public · Anyone can attend

The Icons of Change Metro Manila 2025 Awarding Ceremony is set to spotlight exemplary leadership, innovation, and societal transformation in an event that promises to redefine how change is celebrated. This prestigious annual program honors individuals, organizations, and businesses that have consistently driven positive societal impact through their work. By recognizing not only established leaders but also the unsung heroes whose contributions often go unnoticed, the awards aim to elevate a culture of excellence, social responsibility, and visionary change.

In Partnership with Metro Manila SDG Action Summit

This year, the Awards will run in tandem with the Metro Manila SDG Action Summit 2025, a dynamic platform aligned with the United Nations' designation of 2025 as the International Year of Peace and Trust. The Summit will serve as an essential forum for dialogue and collaboration among stakeholders, as it demonstrates the vital importance of collective action and community-driven initiatives in advancing the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Event Highlights

The combined events are designed to shine a light on transformative efforts—from eradicating poverty and promoting quality education to advancing gender equality and ensuring environmental sustainability—thereby galvanizing meaningful progress on multiple fronts.

Set for April 27, 2025, from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM at Southville International School and Colleges in Las Piñas City, the event will feature:

- Keynote Speeches: Delivered by distinguished leaders who have shaped the course of social innovation and public policy.

- Award Presentations: Across various SDG-aligned categories celebrating past achievements and inspiring future endeavors.

- Interactive Networking: Sessions designed to promote the exchange of ideas, best practices, and collaborative strategies among changemakers, policymakers, and industry experts.

Beyond the Ceremony

Beyond the awards ceremony itself, the Icons of Change Metro Manila 2025 Awarding Ceremony will document the narratives and success stories of its honorees in a comprehensive publication. This book aims to serve as both an educational resource and a lasting source of inspiration, offering detailed insights into the journeys of those who have dedicated themselves to making a difference.

Our Vision

The Icons of Change Metro Manila 2025 Awarding Ceremony seeks to catalyze a movement of innovation, leadership, and sustainable development. It celebrates those who have dared to challenge the status quo and create meaningful change, reinforcing the idea that every effort—no matter how small—can contribute to a brighter, more equitable future for Metro Manila and beyond.

Wellness Without Borders: Nature's Impact on Your Health

SM Center Las Piñas, Las Piñas City, Philippines

January 18, 2025 | 10:00 AM - 12:00 NN

Event by Icons of Change

Public · Anyone can attend

In an era where health and environmental challenges are ever-evolving, understanding the deep connection between personal wellness and the health of our planet is crucial. Join us for an enlightening seminar titled "Wellness Without Borders: Nature's Impact on Your Health," which will explore how the environment directly influences our physical and mental health.

Distinguished Speakers

Dra. Ma. Victoria Diezmo-Marbibi

Head and Senior Manager of Healthcare Access and Vaccination Services at Asian Hospital and Medical Center

Dra. Diezmo-Marbibi is a highly respected figure in healthcare, with extensive experience in promoting public health initiatives and vaccination programs. She will be discussing the importance of preventive healthcare, especially vaccination, and how a proactive approach to physical health contributes to overall wellness. She will delve into the vital role of healthcare systems in ensuring better access to services that support long-term well-being.

David D'Angelo

Board of Director at Global Responsibility for Environmental and Ecological Nurturing (GREEN)

David D'Angelo, a passionate environmentalist and advocate for sustainable development, will offer his unique perspective on how environmental health impacts our personal health. With his leadership roles as the National President of the Green Party of the Philippines, and Vice President of Bayanihan Para sa Kalikasan Movement, David will explore the interconnectedness of our ecosystems and the human body. He will highlight how climate change, pollution, and ecological degradation directly affect our health, and offer solutions for creating a more sustainable future through collective action.

Why Attend?

This seminar will provide invaluable insights into the growing link between personal wellness and environmental health. Attendees will learn about the critical factors that influence their well-being—ranging from air and water quality to the preservation of natural resources—and how these elements shape our overall health.

This event also aims to encourage proactive steps to improve our surroundings, knowing that a healthier planet means a healthier you. Whether you are a healthcare professional, environmental advocate, or simply someone interested in how nature affects your personal wellness, this event will equip you with the knowledge to take meaningful action in both your health journey and environmental responsibilities.

Join Us for This Vital Discussion!

Be part of the conversation that connects physical health with ecological preservation. Don't miss the chance to hear from these two impactful leaders as they share their expertise on how we can achieve holistic health in harmony with nature.

Together, let's build a healthier future—one that bridges the gap between personal wellness and a thriving planet!

January 11, 2025 | 2:00 PM - 4:30 PM

Event by Icons of Change

Public · Anyone can attend

Join us for an inspiring educational event featuring two distinguished speakers who will share invaluable insights on diabetes management and cultivating genuine compassion in our daily lives.

Featured Speakers

Dr. Arvin C. Marbibi

Specialist in Lifestyle Medicine and Diabetes Management

Dr. Marbibi will present "Understanding Diabetes: Prevention, Management, and Living Well." With his extensive clinical experience, Dr. Marbibi will provide practical knowledge about diabetes prevention, effective management strategies, and how to maintain a fulfilling life while living with this condition. His talk will cover the latest research, lifestyle interventions, and medical approaches that can make a significant difference in managing diabetes and improving overall health.

Malvin Dinlasan

Compassionate Leadership Coach and Community Development Advocate

Malvin will present "The Power of Genuine Compassion in Personal and Professional Relationships." Drawing from his work in community development and leadership coaching, Malvin will explore how authentic compassion can transform our interactions with others, enhance our leadership capabilities, and create more harmonious environments at home and in the workplace. His presentation will include practical exercises and real-world examples of how compassion can be a driving force for positive change.

Why Attend?

This event offers a unique opportunity to gain knowledge that can transform both your physical health and personal relationships. Whether you are directly affected by diabetes, care for someone who is, or simply want to learn preventive measures, Dr. Marbibi's talk will provide valuable information. Similarly, Malvin's insights on compassion can help anyone seeking to improve their personal connections and leadership skills.

Attendees will receive resource materials, have the opportunity to ask questions during dedicated Q&A sessions, and connect with others who share similar interests in health and personal development.

Join Us for This Enriching Experience!

Don't miss this chance to learn from experts who are passionate about empowering others through knowledge and practical wisdom. This event is free and open to the public, but registration is encouraged due to limited seating.

International Day of Persons with Disabilities 2024

SM City Las Piñas, Las Piñas City, Philippines

December 7, 2024 | 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Event by Icons of Change

Public · Anyone can attend

In celebration of the International Day of Persons with Disabilities (IDPD), Icons of Change presents a special event highlighting the achievements and contributions of people with disabilities, while raising awareness about the importance of creating an inclusive, accessible society for all.

Event Theme: "Building Forward Better: Toward an Inclusive, Accessible, and Sustainable Post-COVID-19 World for Persons with Disabilities"

This year's event focuses on the unique challenges and opportunities that have emerged for persons with disabilities during and after the global pandemic, and how we can create more inclusive communities moving forward.

Featured Speakers

John Guarnes

Disability Rights Advocate and Inclusive Education Specialist

John will share his personal journey and discuss the importance of accessible education for all. His presentation will highlight successful models of inclusive educational settings and provide practical recommendations for schools and institutions to better accommodate students with various disabilities.

Dr. Mayla Wahab

Director, National Council on Disability Affairs

Dr. Wahab will provide insights into national policy frameworks supporting persons with disabilities and discuss how government, private sector, and civil society can collaborate to create more accessible physical and digital environments. Her talk will include the latest data on disability inclusion in the Philippines and progress in implementing the UN Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities.

Event Highlights

- Accessible Arts Showcase: Featuring artwork, performances, and creations by talented individuals with disabilities

- Technology Demonstrations: Exhibiting the latest assistive technologies and digital accessibility tools

- Inclusive Employment Forum: Exploring opportunities and best practices for workplace inclusion

- Community Resource Fair: Connecting attendees with local services and support organizations

Why Attend?

This event offers valuable insights for:

- Persons with disabilities and their families seeking resources and community

- Educators and employers interested in creating more inclusive environments

- Policymakers and community leaders focused on accessibility and inclusion

- Anyone committed to supporting equal rights and opportunities for all people

Join Us in Celebrating Diversity and Inclusion!

Together, we can break down barriers and build a more inclusive society that values the contributions of all its members. This event is free and open to the public, with full accessibility accommodations available.
