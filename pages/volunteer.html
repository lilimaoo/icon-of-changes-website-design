Why Volunteer With Us

At Icons of Change, volunteers are the heartbeat of our organization. By joining our volunteer team, you become part of a vibrant community of passionate individuals dedicated to creating lasting, positive change in communities across the Philippines and beyond.

Whether you have a few hours to spare or are looking for a long-term volunteer opportunity, your skills, experience, and enthusiasm can make a real difference in the lives of others.

Volunteer Opportunities

Find the perfect role that matches your skills and passion

Event Support

Help organize and facilitate community events, workshops, and recognition ceremonies. Assist with setup, registration, coordination, and ensuring participants have a great experience.

Short-term, event-based
On-site

Mentorship Programs

Share your expertise and experience by mentoring community leaders, youth changemakers, or program participants. Guide them through challenges and help them develop leadership skills.

3-6 month commitment
In-person or virtual

Digital & Creative Support

Use your digital, design, photography, or content creation skills to help tell the stories of community changemakers and amplify our mission through compelling communications.

Flexible, project-based
Remote

Community Project Support

Work directly with community initiatives to implement projects, conduct research, or provide specialized skills that help address local challenges and build community capacity.

Varied commitment
On-site

Administrative Support

Help our organization run smoothly by assisting with administrative tasks, database management, correspondence, volunteer coordination, and logistical planning.

Regular, ongoing
Office-based or remote

Advisory Roles

Ongoing, periodic meetings
Remote or in-person
