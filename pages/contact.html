Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Icons of Change, our programs, and how you can get involved.

How can I become a volunteer with Icons of Change?
To volunteer with Icons of Change, visit our Volunteer Opportunities page and fill out the application form. Once submitted, our team will review your details and invite you to an orientation session. Depending on your role, training may be provided before you start assisting in events, mentorship, or community projects. For more details, contact us at theiconsofchange@gmail.com.
How can I nominate someone for an Icons of Change award?
To nominate someone for an Icons of Change award, visit our Nominations page and complete the nomination form with details about the nominee and their contributions to community development. Our selection committee reviews all nominations quarterly. Nominees should demonstrate leadership, innovation, and measurable impact in their communities.
What types of programs does Icons of Change offer?
Icons of Change offers various programs including leadership development workshops, community project funding, social entrepreneurship training, environmental sustainability initiatives, and youth empowerment programs. We also provide networking opportunities through our annual summit and regular community gatherings. Check our Programs page for current offerings and upcoming schedules.
How can I support Icons of Change?
You can support Icons of Change in multiple ways: volunteer your time and skills, become a financial contributor, partner with us as an organization, donate resources or equipment, provide mentorship to our community members, or help spread awareness about our mission. Visit our Support page to learn more about how you can make a difference.
Where can I find updates on upcoming events?
For the latest updates on our upcoming events, you can follow us on social media platforms, subscribe to our monthly newsletter, or regularly check the Events section on our website. We also send email notifications to our community members about important events and opportunities to participate.
