Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2020

At Icons of Change, your privacy isn't just respected—it's foundational.

We understand the value of trust in a digitally-first world. This website exists solely to share powerful stories, celebrate changemakers, and inspire. As such, we do not collect personal information from visitors.

What We Collect

Absolutely nothing that can personally identify you. Our digital platform is intentionally designed for passive browsing, with no data fields, user accounts, or analytics tools that track individuals.

Why This Matters

Icons of Change is a mission-driven initiative dedicated to recognizing and empowering everyday trailblazers who create meaningful, lasting impact in communities. By ensuring accessible and anonymous browsing experiences, we uphold our values of respect, empowerment, and integrity.

Third-Party Content

Occasionally, our pages may embed videos, social media links, or external articles. These platforms may operate under different privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies for clarity.

Security

While we don't collect your data, we still take website security seriously. Our platform is hosted and maintained with industry-standard safeguards to prevent unauthorized access or tampering.

Updates to This Policy

If our website functionality changes in the future—for example, introducing subscriptions or contact forms—we will transparently update this policy and honor your rights and digital dignity.

Icons of Change envisions a world where leaders are empowered to drive sustainable transformation—boldly, visibly, unapologetically. Your privacy is part of that commitment.

For questions or clarification, you can contact us at theiconsofchange@gmail.com
